About JSIXRON

One supplier for the things that keep a business running.

JSIXRON Consumer Products & Merchandise operates out of 208 Bagong Pagasa, Delpan, Binondo, Manila — the trading heart of the city — as a single-window supplier for general merchandise, industrial materials, corporate branding, and logistics support.

Instead of routing every requirement through a different vendor, clients bring it straight to Ron, who sources, quotes, and coordinates fulfillment personally, while Jerald runs day-to-day operations — production timelines, hauling schedules, and delivery — so orders move from confirmed to completed without stalling.
